## Tokenomics Overview

InterLink Token is the native digital asset of the InterLink Network — designed to become the most widely distributed and human-owned token in the world.

Unlike traditional cryptocurrencies — and even Bitcoin, which favors those with early capital or mining hardware — InterLink Token is earned exclusively by real, verified humans through proof of personhood. One person, one node, one opportunity to participate. No rigs, no bots, no manipulation.

Where Bitcoin proves computational power, InterLink Token proves humanity.

Vision: onboard 1 billion real people → become the most decentralized cryptocurrency in terms of human distribution — powering governance, applications, and the on-chain reputation economy.

## InterLink Token And InterLink Genesis

The InterLink Token ($ITL) and InterLink Genesis ($ITLG) models are inspired by Bitcoin and Ethereum, respectively.

- **$ITL** serves as a reserve asset for businesses and stakeholders — primarily used for staking to access the InterLink Human Layer.
- **$ITLG** functions as the utility token within the broader InterLink ecosystem.

## Token Mining Mechanism and Sustainability

In the early stages, mining is designed to be accessible and straightforward → encourages mass adoption, rapid onboarding, and strengthening the human node network.

### Key Mechanisms
1. **Balanced Reward Structure**: New users are not left too far behind in token acquisition, while early adopters maintain strong incentives to continue mining actively.
2. **Anti-Bot Protection**: Real human verification through InterLink ID → bot activities fully mitigated → only genuine users participate in mining rewards.
3. **Token Locking Mechanism**: A proportion of mined tokens is subject to strategic vesting and locking schedules → healthy token supply-demand balance → prevents excessive immediate circulation.
4. **Equitable Profit Opportunities**: Both small and large token holders can achieve favorable returns without disproportionately affecting the overall token price.

InterLink's architecture guarantees that token inflation is tightly controlled through real human verification, vesting mechanisms, and a carefully calibrated reward model.

**Mining accessibility today, sustainable value for tomorrow.**

## FAQ (from Whitepaper)

### Q: When will the token be listed on an exchange? What will the TGE look like?
The current plan is to list the token toward the end of 2025 or early 2026. However, now that we have the InterLink DAO, all major decisions will be made transparently with the community. The final decision will be made through a vote by $ITLG holders.

For TGE: the system will calculate the token unlock schedule based on a linear relationship with the number of tokens held — with a maximum vesting period of up to 180 months. The more tokens one holds, the longer the lock-up period, ensuring price stability and sustainable growth.

### Q: Will the token face excessive inflation? Are there still more halving events planned?
The token will be burned and used as a medium of exchange throughout the entire ecosystem. Additionally, bots and farmers have been completely eliminated. Halving events will continue — potentially up to 100 times — to ensure consistent reduction in emissions and long-term value preservation.

### Q: Does the dual-token model ($ITL and $ITLG) risk diluting overall value?
No — the dual-token model was carefully designed to separate governance, utility, and strategic functions:
- **$ITLG**: Genesis token, earned exclusively through mining by verified humans. Governs InterLink DAO. Critical role in expanding the human network + future decentralized payment layer.
- **$ITL**: Core utility and strategic token, structured for integration with ecosystem partners, institutions, and large-scale capital. Aligned with future public offerings or stock listings — regulatory-friendly, institution-ready model.

### Q: What will the $ITLG token price be at listing?
InterLink has its own internal formula to determine a fair valuation — directly proportional to the number of users in the network.

### Q: Why was ITLG initially said to be 10 billion? Did you increase the supply?
No. The total supply of InterLink Token ($ITL) has always been fixed at 10 billion and has never changed. $ITLG supply was also transparently shared from the beginning with partners, investors, and ecosystem stakeholders including Google, AWS, NIST, and NYSE-aligned institutions.
