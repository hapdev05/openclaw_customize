# Interlink Ambassador Program
> Source: Official Interlink Ambassador Program Guidelines (Google Docs)
> Last updated: 2026-04-03

---

## 1. Introduction

The Ambassador Program is a long-term ecosystem of contribution and growth, serving as a bridge between InterLink and local communities worldwide.

---

## 2. Tier System

| Tier | Level |
|---|---|
| **Tier 3** | Entry level — new ambassadors start here |
| **Tier 2** | Mid-level — proven performers |
| **Tier 1** | Senior level — high impact |
| **Global Partner** | Top level — exceptional influence |
| **Core Team** | Highest level — special selection with increased responsibilities and benefits |

---

## 3. How to Become an Ambassador

1. **Onboarding**: Join the InterLink Coach House Onboarding Telegram group: https://t.me/InterLinkCoachHouse
2. **Training**: 7-day guided training under a Coach
3. **Assignment**: Evaluation for entry into Tier 3, official badge on the app, and creation of an Ambassador Profile at https://ambassador.interlinklabs.ai
4. **Involvement**: Active participation in ecosystem activities

---

## 4. Responsibilities

- Maintain a public Ambassador Profile (mandatory for earning ACS)
- Complete Daily Tasks (social media content) and Monthly Tasks (high-value contributions)
- Focus on accuracy, community support, and brand alignment
- Avoid over-hyping and low-quality AI-generated content

---

## 5. Rewards & Benefits

- **$ITLG Allocations**: Direct token rewards and cash for top performers
- **ACS (Ambassador Contribution Score)**: The primary metric for trust and additional token eligibility
- **Growth**: Early access to features and direct connection to the Core Team

---

## 6. MVP Recognition

Criteria: Creating valuable content, inviting new members, mentoring others, and collaborating on events (AMAs, workshops).

---

## 7. Daily Tasks

- **Duration**: 5–10 days per topic
- **Requirements**: Minimum of 10 related content links per task
- **Submission**: Via form: https://forms.gle/H6CdrpFz2nGproHB6

### Point System

| Platform | Activity | Points |
|---|---|---|
| **Telegram** | Hosting AMAs/Quizzes | 10 pts |
| **X (Twitter)** | Reposts | 1 pt |
| **X (Twitter)** | Basic posts | 5 pts |
| **X (Twitter)** | Analytical articles | 8 pts |
| **Facebook/Insta/Threads** | Articles | 5 pts |
| **Facebook/Insta/Threads** | High-quality Reels | 7 pts |
| **TikTok/YouTube** | Basic images | 3 pts |
| **TikTok/YouTube** | Standard videos | 5 pts |
| **TikTok/YouTube** | High-quality analytical (voice-over, >30s) | 8 pts |

**Performance Bonuses**: Extra points (+1 to +10) for high engagement (views, likes, participants).

---

## 8. Monthly Rewards & Exchange Rates

| Tier | Rate | Monthly Cap |
|---|---|---|
| **Tier 3** | 1 Point = 5 ITLG | 500 ITLG |
| **Tier 2** | 1 Point = 10 ITLG | 1,000 ITLG |
| **Tier 1** | 1 Point = 15 ITLG | 1,500 ITLG |
| **Global Partner** | 1 Point = 20 ITLG | 2,000 ITLG |

---

## 9. Mentorship & Referral Rewards

- **Mentorship**: 100 points per mentee who successfully levels up
- **Referral (10% Commission)**:
  - **Inviter**: 10% commission on the trainee's monthly rewards
  - **Leader (Manager)**: 10% commission on the trainee's monthly rewards
  - **Combined**: If you are both Inviter and Leader, you earn 20%

---

## 10. Penalties for Poor Management

Leaders incur deductions if team members are removed for poor performance or inactivity:
- **Tier 2 Leaders**: -5% of total monthly reward per member removed
- **Tier 1 Leaders**: -10% of total monthly reward per member removed

## Q&A — Ambassador Program

**Q: How do I become an Ambassador? / Làm sao để trở thành Ambassador?**
> 🇬🇧 Join the InterLink Coach House on Telegram (https://t.me/InterLinkCoachHouse), complete 7-day training under a Coach, then get evaluated for Tier 3 entry. You'll receive an official badge and Ambassador Profile.
>
> 🇻🇳 Tham gia InterLink Coach House trên Telegram (https://t.me/InterLinkCoachHouse), hoàn thành 7 ngày training với Coach, sau đó được đánh giá vào Tier 3. Bạn sẽ nhận badge chính thức và Ambassador Profile.

**Q: What are the Ambassador tiers? / Các cấp bậc Ambassador là gì?**
> There are 5 tiers: Tier 3 (entry) → Tier 2 → Tier 1 → Global Partner → Core Team. Each tier has higher rewards and responsibilities.

**Q: How much ITLG can an Ambassador earn? / Ambassador kiếm được bao nhiêu ITLG?**
> Depends on tier: Tier 3 = max 500 ITLG/month (1pt=5 ITLG), Tier 2 = max 1,000 ITLG (1pt=10), Tier 1 = max 1,500 ITLG (1pt=15), Global Partner = max 2,000 ITLG (1pt=20).

**Q: What tasks do Ambassadors do? / Ambassador làm gì?**
> Daily tasks include creating social media content (posts, videos, AMAs, quizzes). Each task runs 5-10 days and requires minimum 10 content links. Submit via: https://forms.gle/H6CdrpFz2nGproHB6

**Q: How does the point system work? / Hệ thống điểm hoạt động như thế nào?**
> Points vary by platform and quality: Telegram AMAs = 10pts, Twitter posts = 5pts, YouTube high-quality videos = 8pts, TikTok basic = 3pts. Extra +1 to +10 bonus for high engagement.

**Q: What is ACS? / ACS là gì?**
> ACS (Ambassador Contribution Score) is the primary metric for trust and additional token eligibility. Maintaining a public Ambassador Profile is mandatory for earning ACS.

**Q: Do Ambassadors get referral rewards? / Ambassador có thưởng giới thiệu không?**
> Yes. Inviter gets 10% commission on trainee's monthly rewards. Leader gets another 10%. If you are both Inviter and Leader = 20%. Plus 100 bonus points per mentee who levels up.

**Q: What happens if my team performs poorly? / Team hoạt động kém thì sao?**
> Leaders get penalized: Tier 2 Leaders lose -5% of total monthly reward per member removed. Tier 1 Leaders lose -10% per member removed.

**Q: Where is the Ambassador profile? / Ambassador profile ở đâu?**
> https://ambassador.interlinklabs.ai — You must maintain this profile to earn ACS.

**Q: Contact for Ambassador concerns? / Liên hệ Ambassador?**
> Contact @ekwinbudi on Telegram for Ambassador-related concerns.
