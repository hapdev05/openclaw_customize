## 3. INTERLINK ECOSYSTEM

- **InterLink ID**: A globally verified human identity system using facial biometrics and Liveness detection to ensure "one person, one account" without compromising privacy.
- **InterLink App**: The primary gateway for users to manage their identity, participate in mining, and access the ecosystem's services.
- **InterLink SDK & MDK**: Tools for developers to integrate InterLink's human verification and payment systems into their own applications (Mini-apps).

- **ITLX Super Wallet**: A non-custodial wallet integrated with InterLink ID, supporting gasless transactions and multiple blockchain networks.

## 6. HUMAN NODES

- **Definition**: Every verified human acts as a "Human Node" in the network.
- **Function**: Nodes verify other nodes through a decentralized consensus, making the network resilient to bots and Sybil attacks.
- **Rewards**: Users earn rewards based on their verification status and network participation.

## 9. HUMAN CREDIT SYSTEM (HCS)

- **The First AI-Powered Credit System**: Uses Graph Neural Networks (GNN) to analyze network behavior as a proxy for creditworthiness.
- **Verification Path**: Users improve their HCS through consistent mining, security group participation, and community contributions.
- **Impact**: A high HCS unlocks higher reward tiers, better $ITLG allocations, and priority access to new features.

---

## 10. BURNING & RECOVERY MECHANISMS

- **Deflationary Model**: A portion of $ITL used in the ecosystem is permanently burned to reduce supply.
- **$ITLG Recovery Burning**: A mechanism designed to stabilize the value of the governance token by burning recovered or unused tokens from the ecosystem treasury.

---

## 11. INDIVIDUAL VS GROUP MINING

- **Individual Mining**: Focuses on scalability and solo verification rewards.
- **Group Mining (Security Groups)**:
  - Groups of up to 5 verified Human Nodes
  - **Security Groups** utilize multi-node consensus to strengthen the network's Proof of Personhood
  - **Rewards**: Higher rewards for groups with consistent activity and high-trust members
  - **Secure Badge**: Earned by fully active groups, significantly boosting members' Human Credit Score (HCS)
